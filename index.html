Where songs come from

Songs: streaming apps (Spotify, an Apple Music subscription) don't give you files you can upload — you need the actual audio file. Buying the track (e.g. the iTunes Store) and saving it to your phone's Files works great, as does anything you make in GarageBand.

Announcements: record in the Voice Memos app, tap Share → Save to Files, then add it here. Recording a kid's name + number takes 10 seconds.

Tip: you only need the good 15–20 seconds of a song — after uploading, use the trim editor to cut to the best part.

Share with coaches

Get every coach's phone on the same soundboard. Create a team code, text it over, and joining downloads everything automatically — changes keep syncing both ways after that.

Backup

A spare copy, not for sharing — everything (songs, lineup, colors, logo) in one file. Keep it in your Files app in case this phone is lost or replaced, and restore it with Import.
